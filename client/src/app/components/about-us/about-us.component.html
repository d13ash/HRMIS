About Us

The Knowledge and Technology Resource Centre (KTRC) at Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya, Raipur, supported by RKVY, Govt. of India, empowers students, farmers, and communities in Chhattisgarh through innovative e-governance in agriculture.

KTRC features a cutting-edge Digital KISAN Studio with real-time video production and broadcasting, offering expert guidance and best practices tailored for students and farmers.

More than a facility, KTRC represents a commitment to digital transformation, knowledge sharing, and continuous learning in agriculture.

" Empowering agriculture through innovation, technology, and knowledge sharing for a sustainable future. "
